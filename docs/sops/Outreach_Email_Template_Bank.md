## 2. How to use the bank

1. Short message for a generic inbox/contact form; the master email for a named dealer-program contact.
2. Research first: one product family/model + one genuine reason it fits FDS customers.
3. Replace every placeholder. 4. One clear CTA (application, decision-maker, or short call). 5. Sensitive docs only to verified company-domain email or secure portal. 6. Log rep/status/MAP/fulfillment/follow-up date in the CRM.

**Personalization formula:** "We have researched [Brand] and are particularly interested in your [specific product family/model]. We believe the line would be a strong fit for our customers because [specific reason connected to the product, customer, or use case]."

## 7. Reply and application templates (7–13)

- **T7 — Supplier expresses interest / asks who we are:** thank them; identity block (Zeto International LLC, Cedar Hill TX; store, website, address, Ben's contact); marketing strategy (Shopping/Search/SEO/email/financing/phone support); "accurate product information, clear compatibility guidance, transparent freight expectations, MAP compliance"; ask for the application + which documents and whether by email or secure portal. *Say "building" until fully live; "operates" after.*
- **T8 — Dealer application cover email:** attach the completed application; list ONLY documents actually attached (resale certificate, EIN confirmation, formation document, W-9 as requested); "delete every document line that is not actually attached."
- **T9 — Pitch deck cover email:** the partnership overview bullets (who we are, customers/market, why [Brand] fits, how we market and support, benefits); ask for an intro call. Personalize the deck for Tier A.
- **T10 — Not accepting online dealers:** thank them; ask whether the restriction is all dealers / online-only / territory / product line; ask for the authorized distributor; ask to be kept on file.
- **T11 — Showroom / stocking / sales-history requirements:** ask for the exact opening order or annual minimum, mixed-product eligibility, display standards, sales history, payment terms, and whether a probationary/referral/lead-gen/non-stocking route exists.
- **T12 — Request the correct distributor / territory rep:** ask for an introduction to the distributor, manufacturer rep, or territory manager for Texas + online U.S. accounts.
- **T13 — Respectful decline or postponement:** thank them, name the requirement we can't support properly, ask permission to reconnect in [month/quarter] after [milestone].

## 8. Qualification and onboarding emails (14–17)

- **T14 — Program qualification questions** (after interest, never in the first cold email): dealer approval process/territory/channel · approved online SKUs, best sellers, restricted products · dealer cost, MAP, promotions, price-update process · opening/per-order minimums, payment terms, card/ACH fees · direct-to-customer fulfillment, warehouses, handling time, inventory feed, backorders · freight quoting, residential/liftgate charges, damage claims, inspection, redelivery · returns/warranty workflows, restocking, freight responsibility · approved images/manuals/videos/data, trademark use, Google Shopping + brand-keyword rules.
- **T15 — Approved dealer onboarding asset request:** price list + MAP, approved catalog/SKUs, images/videos/manuals/specs/warranty docs, inventory feed + schedule, freight quote process, order submission + contacts, returns/damage/parts/warranty procedures, brand guidelines, training resources; confirm launch date + whether pages need pre-publication review.
- **T16 — Product/configuration clarification** (before publishing anything disputed): list the specification/compatibility/freight questions, name our current source (catalog/manual + date), "we will not publish the disputed detail until it is confirmed." *(This is the Import SOP's accuracy gate in email form.)*
- **T17 — Re-engagement after a milestone:** reference the earlier conversation + the named milestone, state it's now met, ask to restart the dealer review.

## 9. Attachment and document-security policy

- **First contact:** usually website link only. Pitch deck + Texas resale certificate optional for a *verified dealer-program contact*. Never: EIN confirmation, formation documents, W-9, banking or credit info.
- **Secure-document sentence:** "We can provide our company presentation, Texas resale certificate, EIN confirmation, formation documents, and any other required information through your preferred secure application process."
- **Rule:** sensitive documents only to a verified company-domain address or secure supplier portal — never an unverified personal email.
- **Standard file names:** `Farmer_Direct_Supply_Dealer_Presentation.pdf` · `Farmer_Direct_Supply_Texas_Resale_Certificate.pdf` · `Zeto_International_LLC_EIN_Confirmation.pdf` · `Zeto_International_LLC_Formation_Document.pdf` · `Farmer_Direct_Supply_W9.pdf`
- **Workspace location:** the signed resale certificate lives locally at `assets/business/Farmer_Direct_Supply_Texas_Resale_Certificate.pdf` (gitignored — signed documents never enter version control).
